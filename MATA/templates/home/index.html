Matadata-based Search Engine

Our searching engine has three different types of ranking method:

Standard: search the query only consider the relativity of the item title and description with equal weight

Comment matter: combine with the standard ranking method, this method consider other user's review as a factor.

Mega: the ranking method will distinguish the positive review and negative review and give different weight, also consider the rating score from user and combine with corresponding review to determine the weight.

*The dataset of this project is from Beauty Department in amazon.com
