Matadata-based Search Engine Project

E-commerce platforms are growing at an unprecedented speed all over the globe, so are the varieties of products. In order to make rational decisions and buy good quality products, shoppers tend to spend hours searching for ideal items, reading descriptions, and especially reading other shoppers’ reviews, which implies that the ranking algorithms sometimes cannot provided them with satisfied results. Even worse, when shoppers do not have a clear idea on what they need, their descriptive query might not guarantee them with ideal products. As a result, aiming to improve user shopping experience, we decide to build a search engine, Mega, that would search for products based on their titles, ratings, descriptions, and especially shoppers’ reviews. Different weights will be given to these aspects, and a relevancy score based on bag-of-words matching will be used to determine the final ranking. Besides, relevant reviews will also be displayed beside the products to make the ranking more reasonable. If time allows, we would also implement sentence-level sentiment analysis and topic model to improve the evaluation of relevancy score.
